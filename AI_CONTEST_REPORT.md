# ZapWallet AI Contest Report

This report documents the extensive use of AI tools and methodologies in the redesign and development of ZapWallet.

## 🚀 Accomplishments

### 🎨 Home and Public Pages Design
Updated the entire public-facing UI (About, Features, FAQ, Contact) with a unified, modern brand language.
- Integrated a custom `SectionHeader` component across all routes.
- Optimized the landing page by removing redundancy and focusing on the core value proposition.

### 💬 AI Chatbot Integration
Implemented a sophisticated AI Chatbot within the ZapWallet platform to provide real-time assistance and intelligent financial insights to users.

### 🛡️ Dashboard Design Redesign
The internal user dashboard was completely overhauled to provide a premium, data-driven experience.
- **Balance Page**: Rebuilt with modern cards and interactive balance visibility.
- **Profile Page**: Redesigned with a focus on identity and transaction history.
- **Security**: Updated PIN management and security status visualization.

## 🛠️ AI Toolstack & Methodology

- **Claude & Stitch**: Leveraged for high-fidelity UI/UX design updates of the Dashboard and the foundational `SectionHeader` component.
- **ChatGPT**: Utilized for iterative research, feature planning, and content strategy.
- **DeepSeek**: Instrumental in the technical implementation and optimization of the real-time AI chat application.
- **Antigravity (by Google DeepMind)**: The primary Code Editor and AI agent used to orchestrate the entire development lifecycle, from planning to complex multi-file refactoring and verification.
